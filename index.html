About Jay Davis

Jay Davis is a junior developer from Lower Hutt in the windy capital of New Zealand. He has a passion for renewable energy products and electric vehicles and privately imported Wellingtons first Nissan Leaf EV.
He lives with his fiancee Lucy in their home overlooking the fantastic views of the harbour.
Jay runs 2 companies and loves the challenges almost as much as the amazing people he gets to meet on a daily basis.
In his spare time Jay volounteers at the sustainable living centre in Lower Hutt and also enjoys going for walks up along Wellingtons many great bike/walking tracks. Visit my FULL website @ jay-davis.info
